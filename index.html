The timeline of Jesus's life

  • 6BC approx. - Jesus’ birth
  • 4 BC approx. - Wise men visit and Jesus escapes to Egypt
  • 8 AD approx. - JJesus’ childhood – left behind in Jerusalem
  • 28 AD approx. - Jesus is baptised and tempted by the devil
  • 28 AD approx. - Jesus recruits disciples and performs first miracle
  • 28AD to 30AD approx. - Jesus begins teaching and miraculoushealings
  • 30 AD approx. - Jesus’ arrest, crucifixion and resurrection
